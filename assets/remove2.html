Code:

$git rm neel*.txt

Explaination:

Removes all *.txt files from the index that are under the neel directory and any of its subdirectories. Note that the asterisk * is quoted from the shell in this example; this lets Git, and not the shell, expand the pathnames of files and subdirectories under the neel/ directory.

Code:

$git rm -f git-*.sh

Because this example lets the shell expand the asterisk (i.e. you are listing the files explicitly), it does not remove subdir/git-foo.sh.
